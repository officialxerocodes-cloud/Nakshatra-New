Spacecon

SpaceCon is the annual space science and technology conclave of NSUT, proudly organized by Nakshatra, The Astronomy and Mathematics Society, in collaboration with The Debugging Society. This three-day festival is a celebration of innovation and research in the fields of space science and technology, offering a platform for participants to showcase their skills, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.

With an exciting lineup of events, including hackathons, ideathons, case study challenges, quizzes, Gen AI challenges, and astrophotography competitions, will engage and inspire the participants . Beyond competitions, participants can gain invaluable insights through captivating speaker sessions, guest lectures, and podcasts featuring renowned experts in these domains.

Join us at SpaceCon to explore the cosmos, connect with like-minded enthusiasts, and take a step closer to unraveling the mysteries of the universe.
